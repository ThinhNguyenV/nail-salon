# 🇩🇪 Fortuna Beauty: German Market Branding & SKILL

This document defines the core standards for the **Fortuna Beauty** project, focusing on a premium experience for German customers.

## 🎨 Design & UI Standards (Premium Aesthetic)

The UI must feel **expensive, clean, and professional**. Avoid standard red/blue/green.

- **Color Palette (Modern HSL):**
    - **Primary:** Deep Rose/Mauve (#8E5D5D) or Slate Gold for a high-end feel.
    - **Background:** Dark Mode (Deep Charcoal) or "Glassmorphism" light mode.
    - **Accents:** Subtle gradients and smooth micro-animations on hover.
- **Typography:**
    - **Headings:** "Cormorant Garamond" (Elegant Serif) – used for a luxury feel.
    - **Body:** "Poppins" or "Inter" (Clean Sans-Serif) – for high readability.
    - **Special:** "Great Vibes" for signature elements or logo-like text.
- **Aesthetics:** 
    - Use smooth transitions (`transition: 0.3s ease`).
    - High-quality photography from `assets/images`.
    - No generic placeholders.

## 📝 Content Guidelines (Professional German)

All text must use **Formal/Professional German** (*Sie-form* if applicable, though *Du-form* is acceptable for modern salons).

### Core Vocabulary (Dienstleistungen):
- **Nagelmodellage:** Nail construction/modeling.
- **Maniküre / Shellac:** Manicure services.
- **Pediküre & Zehenmodellage:** Pedicure and toe modeling.
- **Medizinische Fußpflege:** Medical foot care (very important in Germany).
- **Sonstige:** Extras/Others (Nail art, removal, etc.).

### Tone of Voice:
- **Quality-Focused:** Emphasize hygiene, precision, and beauty.
- **Clear Pricing:** In Germany, customers expect transparent pricing (Prices page).
- **Easy Contact:** Direct CTAs to WhatsApp or Booking systems.

## 🛠 Project Structure (WordPress)

- **Landing Page:** `front-page.php`.
- **Key Templates:** `page-gallery.php`, `page-prices.php`, `page-contact.php`.
- **Assets:** `assets/css/custom-style.css`, `assets/js/custom-script.js`.
- **Automation:** `functions.php` (Auto-creates pages and sets permalinks on activation).
